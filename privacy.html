Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 13, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Flow: Focus & Screen Time. Your privacy is not just a feature; it's our core principle. This policy explains how VirelonSoft handles your data when you use our application.

Data Sovereignty

Strict Privacy Rule: Flow operates on a "Local-First" basis. Your app usage data, screen time statistics, and personal habits never leave your device.

We do not use any external servers to process your usage stats. All analysis happens locally on your hardware to ensure maximum speed and total privacy.

Information We Process

To provide you with accurate insights, Flow requests access to:

  • Usage Statistics: To track which apps you use and for how long.
  • Notification Access: Only if you enable the Notification Blocker feature.
  • System Settings: To manage Focus and Detox modes.

None of this information is transmitted to VirelonSoft or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

We do not integrate with third-party tracking or advertising SDKs that compromise your anonymity. The app is designed to be a clean, distraction-free environment.

Your Rights

Because your data is stored locally, you have full control over it at all times. You can:

  • Clear all usage history from the app settings.
  • Revoke permissions through your device system settings.
  • Export your data for personal backup.
